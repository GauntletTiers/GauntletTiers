Frequently Asked Questions

How does the tier system work?

Our tier system ranges from HT1 (Highest Tier 1 - Best) to LT5 (Lowest Tier 5 - Worst). Players are ranked based on their performance in official tournaments. HT1 and LT1 represent the elite players, while HT5 and LT5 are for beginners.

How do I get ranked?

To get ranked, you need to participate in our official tournaments. Join our Discord server to find upcoming tournaments and registration details. After participating in at least 3 tournaments, you'll receive an initial tier placement.

Can I appeal my tier placement?

Yes, tier appeals can be submitted through the admin panel or by contacting support. You must provide evidence of recent tournament performances that justify a tier change. Appeals are reviewed weekly.

How often are tier lists updated?

Tier lists are updated every Sunday based on the previous week's tournament results. Major updates may occur after large tournaments or season finals.

What game modes are supported?

We currently support rankings for: Sword PvP, Axe PvP, UHC (Ultra Hardcore), Pot PvP, Crystal PvP, and SMP PvP. Each mode has its own separate tier list.
