About Gauntlet Tiers

Gauntlet Tiers is a community-driven ranking system for Minecraft PvP players. We host regular tournaments across multiple game modes including Sword, Axe, UHC, Pot, Crystal, and SMP PvP.

Our ranking system uses a 10-tier structure from HT1 (highest) to LT5 (entry level), giving players clear goals to work towards and accurate skill representation.

Whether you're a seasoned competitive player or just starting out, our community welcomes everyone. Join our Discord to participate in ranked matches and climb the tiers!
