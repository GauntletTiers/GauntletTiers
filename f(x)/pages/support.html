Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get ranked?
To get ranked, join our Discord server and participate in ranked tournaments. Our staff will evaluate your performance and assign you a tier based on your results.
What do HT and LT mean?
HT stands for "High Tier" and LT stands for "Low Tier". Each tier number (1-5) has a high and low subdivision. HT1 is the highest possible rank, while LT5 is the entry level.
Can I improve my tier?
Yes! Tiers are updated weekly based on tournament performance. Win matches consistently to climb the rankings. You can also request a tier review if you believe your current tier doesn't reflect your skill.
Which game modes are ranked?
We currently rank players in six game modes: Sword, Axe, UHC, Pot, Crystal, and SMP PvP. You can have different tiers in different modes.
